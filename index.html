Who We Are

Building
Engineers.

M&P Robotix is a competitive robotics coaching program operating under the Kedeseen Development Center (KDC) in Alexandria, Egypt.

We train young students to design, build, and program robots for regional and national competitions — developing real engineering skills through hands-on challenges.

Our coaches guide students through VEX IQ, LEGO EV3, and Open category events. Every season brings a new challenge and a new version of each student.

VEX IQ

Team-based robotics using the VEX IQ platform. Students design, build, and program robots that compete in seasonal game-based challenges emphasising teamwork and engineering design.

Teamwork

LEGO

LEGO robotics competitions using EV3 and Spike platforms. Students code autonomous behaviours — line-following, object manipulation, maze solving — and tune their robots to perform reliably under pressure.

Autonomous

No platform restrictions. Students choose their own hardware and software, encouraging creative engineering, cross-disciplinary thinking, and true ownership of their machines.

M&P Robotix is a proud program of the Kedeseen Development Center — a youth development hub in Alexandria offering arts, music, and STEM education to the community.

Within KDC's broader mission, our robotics academy provides a focused, competition-ready track where students train seriously and represent Alexandria on the national stage.

KDC's multi-disciplinary environment means our students don't just build robots — they also grow through creativity, collaboration, and communication across all the center's programmes.
